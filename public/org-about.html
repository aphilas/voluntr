About Us

There is a significant gap in the number of workers that provide social services such as health and education in Kenya. There is a need to bolster the workforce outside of the already strained official channels. Volunteerism can chip in to alleviate this problem. Additionally, research has shown life-long benefits to the mental health of volunteers in non-extreme conditions.

Voluntr enables you to search for voluntr-ing opportunities in Kenya. Go home and make a difference in your community.
